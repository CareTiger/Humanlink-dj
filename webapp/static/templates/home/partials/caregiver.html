Care providers

Humanlink is a mobile and desktop app that enables compassionate groups in the caring process for individuals who are terminally ill or chronically ill or disabled.

Humanlink enables care providers to better manage their time and commute. Our mobile and desktop app will help you save time and money.

Desktop and Mobile

Phone calls and emails get in the way of a productive day. The Humanlink platform enables care professionals to reclaim time.
