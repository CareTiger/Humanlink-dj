Our Story

My name is Ven. I am the Founder and CEO of Humanlink. I live in Rogers, Arkansas. I started Humanlink in my garage about two years ago to solve a personal problem that I was facing.

My Grandma, Savitri (seated in the picture above), lives in India and is 89 years old. She is bed ridden. My mom, Kalyani (green saree above), 68, is the primary caregiver for my grandmother. When I noticed a rapid and non-natural deterioration in my mom's health (heart problems, sleeplessness, weight problems, peripheral edema, etc.) as a result of the pressures of caregiving I decided to do something about it.

Caregiving is very stressful for most aging families and the stress worsens when professional caregivers quit unexpectedly or don't show up. Our customer development in Arkansas included talking to patient families, staffing agencies, caregivers, nursing educators, physicians and medical establishments. We heard consistently of the need for caregiver team coordination via a tool that would alleviate staffing problems, particularly challenges with last minute caregiver no-shows.

How can Humanlink help?

Humanlink is a desktop and mobile messaging app to solve the caregiver no-show problem. In-home staffing schedulers at small and medium for-profit and non-profit enterprises (like churches, area agencies on aging, veterans administration, etc.) who coordinate care for the elderly face this problem. Aside from these enterprises, families who wish to coordinate their own care and staffing for elders also use our app to overcome the no-show problem.

On Humanlink's platform, families and agencies can create private messaging channels for each of their patients. You can list the weekly care needs of the patient in the channel. Families or agencies can invite their existing vetted-and-verified caregivers to join their team on our platform.

You can coordinate care by adding caregivers to each private patient channel based on the caregivers certifications, geographic proximity and availability status. In case of a no-show,you can broadcast a message to your team instead of going through a rolodex and making phone calls or sending out distress emails. In case no caregiver in your rolodex is able to deal with the no-show, Humanlink's discovery feature enables the scheduler to invite verified and vetted independent caregivers from our database to their team.
